At Stealth Digital, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you engage with our services. We prioritize transparency and aim to ensure that your experience with us is secure and respectful of your privacy.

Use of Personal Information by Stealth Digital

At Stealth Digital, we prioritize the privacy and security of our website users. Any personal information collected during registration for educational activities (such as name, date of birth, phone number, and email address) is solely used to deliver services to participants. Children's Privacy

Stealth Digital strictly adheres to privacy regulations and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Marketing & Third-Party Communications

Occasionally, we may use collected information to send you details about programs, products, or services offered by our advertisers. However, we do not sell or rent personal data to unrelated third parties.

Cookie Policy & Privacy Protection

To safeguard your privacy, our website cookies do not store any personal identifying information.

Visitors concerned about cookie usage can adjust their browser settings to:

  • Delete cookies after each session.
  • Block all cookies from being stored.
  • Receive alerts before cookies are saved or exchanged.
Please note that disabling cookies may impact website functionality and result in slower performance.
